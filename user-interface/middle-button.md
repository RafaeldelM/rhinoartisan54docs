# Middle Button

By pressing the wheel on your mouse, a radial menu will open, this is the Middle button menu, where you will find convenient shortcuts to commonly used commands and features.



<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (269).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Clicking any of the icons will run the command it represents, or open a group of related commands.

If you press the cross icon in the center, the Middle Button menu will close.
