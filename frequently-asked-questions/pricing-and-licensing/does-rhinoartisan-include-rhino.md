# Does RhinoArtisan include Rhino?

2Shapes subscriptions don't include Rhino. If you are interested, you can [download the 90-day Rhino trial](https://www.rhino3d.com/download/) or [buy a license](https://www.rhino3d.com/buy).

RhinoArtisan works on Rhino 6.0, Rhino 7.0 and Rhino 8.0.
