### Learn jewelry from our experts on 2Shapes

Jewelry-oriented courses in 3D Modeling, Rendering, SubD, 3D Printing, and much more.

The 2Shapes Jewelry Modeling Academy, available at [academy.2shapes.com](https://academy.2shapes.com), is a comprehensive educational platform designed for individuals interested in learning and honing their skills in jewelry modeling for Rhino and 2Shapes users.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (192).png>)

With a practical approach and step-by-step tutorials, the academy provides a complete learning experience that covers everything from the basics to advanced techniques. Students have access to a wide range of resources, including instructional videos, practical exercises, and assessments, enabling them to acquire solid skills in jewelry design and modeling.&#x20;

Whether you are a beginner taking your first steps into digital jewelry or an experienced professional looking to enhance your skills, the 2Shapes Jewelry Modeling Academy is the ideal place to achieve your goals.

More details at [https://academy.2shapes.com](https://academy.2shapes.com)
